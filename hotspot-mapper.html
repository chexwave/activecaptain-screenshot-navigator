How to use this tool

  1. Add all your known screens to the left sidebar first (or as you go)
  2. Upload a screenshot
  3. Click anywhere on the image to create a hotspot
  4. Choose which screen it should jump to + give it a label
  5. Repeat until all hotspots for that image are mapped
  6. Click "Save This Screen + Hotspots"
  7. Repeat for every screenshot you have
  8. When done, click "Export All Screens JSON" and copy into your main navigator
